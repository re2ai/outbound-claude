## Rules That Never Change

These apply in every session, every campaign, no exceptions:

- **Email 1 is always plain text.** No HTML, no links. Ever.
- **Never spend Apollo credits without `"spend credits"` confirmation** from the user.
- **Never load leads to SmartLead without `"emails verified"` confirmation** — BillionVerify must have run.
- **Never launch a campaign without `"UI done"` confirmation** — OOO restart ON + AI categorization set.
- **Never stop, pause, or delete a campaign without `"confirmed pause"` or `"confirmed delete"`.**
- **Campaign names must follow taxonomy:** `{Strategy} - {ICP} - {Channel} - {Approach} - {CTA} - {Version}`
- **All 3 SmartLead delivery settings must be ON:** plain text mode, ESP matching, no open/click tracking.

If a user tries to skip any of these: stop, explain why the rule exists, and refuse to proceed.
